MOBILE COMMUNICATION

Communication across great distances and without the need for a physical link is made possible by technological means (wires or cables). Being able to communicate on the go simplifies and expedites our daily lives.

FEATURES

HIGH CAPACITY LOAD BALANCING

Load balancing with an adequate capacity ought to be a component of any network, regardless of whether it is wired or wireless. In the case that an access point is too busy to handle the volume of users, high capacity load balancing will relocate those users to an access point with a lower level of congestion automatically.

SCALABILITY

The daily increase in the number of persons who own a wireless device can be thought of as an exponential growth. When it comes to wireless networks, it is possible to start off on a small scale if that is what is required, and then enhance both coverage and capacity as the network's needs rise, all without having to establish an entirely new one or rework the previous one.

ADVANTAGES

FLEXIBILITY: People are able to interact with each other regardless of where they are located since wireless communication is available. It is not necessary to be at a certain location, such as an office or a phone booth, in order to send and receive messages.

COST EFFECTIVENESS: In wireless communication, there is no requirement for any physical infrastructure (wires or cables), hence there is no need for any maintenance practices either. This results in significant cost savings. As a result, the cost is decreased.

SPEED: is another aspect that has experienced significant development and improvement. The precision and speed of the network connectivity, also known as the accessibility, was significantly increased.

ACCESSIBILITY: With the use of wireless technology, it is feasible to achieve accessibility in locations that were previously difficult to reach. For instance, it is now possible to receive an education online even in more rural places. It is no longer necessary for teachers or students to journey to remote locations in order to deliver their lectures.

CONNECTIVITY AT ALL TIMES: Having connectivity at all times enables people to respond to emergencies in a relatively timely manner. For instance, in contrast to a wired landline, a wireless device, such as a mobile phone, can ensure that you have a continuous internet connection even when you move from one location to another or while you are traveling.

DISADVANTAGES

  • As communication is done through open space, it is less secure. Unreliability.
  • More open to interference.
  • Increased chance of jamming.
  • Transmission speed is comparably less.
  • It has a limited amount of bandwidth for communication and breaches of network security.
  • Wireless networks can be easily hacked.

BENEFITS

Assistance in the Event of a Crisis

Having timely, reliable emergency assistance could make all the difference in a disaster. When homeowners and business owners can't reach emergency specialists, it can be dangerous or expensive during natural disasters, personal emergencies, important deliveries, or property maintenance. Mobile communication gives access to local and global information. On this website, homeowners and business owners can obtain a how-to guide for emergency plumbing and the phone number for local services.

Quick and dependable mobile connections let emergency services employees do their jobs more successfully at the scene of an emergency. They can interact with management and outside help through these links.

Make Communicating with Loved Ones and Other People, as well as Others, Easier

Mobile connectivity makes it easier to connect with friends, family, businesses, brands, and other critical services. It allows users to stay updated on news and weather notifications and to build digital groups that strengthen social connections. Mobile phones and social networks enable real-time communication with family, companies, and schools easy.

Improve Productivity for Agricultural Workers and Other Laborers

Agriculture and manual labor are physically taxing, but modern technology has helped alleviate the strain. Mobile communication allows farmworkers to:

  • Boost efficiency and output.
  • Increase the overall quality of your items.
  • Foster safer working conditions at the workplace.
  • Obtain insightful information about the business.

Modern agriculture uses highly advanced gear that allows farmers to collect and analyze data, monitor changes in their environments, identify unsafe amounts of moisture, employ sensors in storage areas, and calculate formulas. Farmers can receive push messages to reorder supplies, contact investors, or inspect crops. Technology and reliable communications help farm businesses network with clients.
